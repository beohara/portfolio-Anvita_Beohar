Wedding App - Vow

Vow is a comprehensive and user-friendly tool that empowers couples to plan and organize their dream wedding with ease. From setting the budget, managing the guest list, designing invitations, booking venues and more - the app streamlines the entire planning journey, putting all the essential tools and resources at their fingertips.

Prototype for Wedding App

Planning a wedding can be incredibly challenging, especially when it comes to keeping track of numerous appointments. With so many tasks and events happening simultaneously, it's easy to feel overwhelmed. To simplify and streamline the process, I created a planner page to help manage all the appointments effectively and keep track of all the bookings.
